ABOUT US

Welcome to Baba Smart Genius and Chess Academy, where brilliance meets strategy! Established in 2006, we embarked on a journey to nurture young minds into ingenious thinkers and strategic masters. Our journey began with the introduction of the abacus, laying the foundation for mental arithmetic and cognitive development. Over the years, we've expanded our horizons, integrating chess into our curriculum, offering a holistic approach to whole brain development. At Baba Smart Genius and Chess Academy, we believe in the power of the abacus as a tool for mental agility and mathematical prowess. The abacus isn't merely a calculating device; it's a gateway to unlocking the potential of the human mind. Through structured abacus training, students sharpen their concentration, enhance memory retention, and cultivate problem-solving skills that extend far beyond mathematics. Our expert instructors guide students through the intricacies of the abacus, fostering a love for numbers and instilling confidence in their abilities.

In addition to the abacus, chess holds a prominent place in our academy, serving as a cornerstone for strategic thinking and analytical reasoning. Chess transcends the boundaries of a mere board game; it's a metaphor for life, where every move carries consequences and every decision shapes the outcome. Through chess, students learn to anticipate, plan, and adapt to dynamic situations, honing their critical thinking skills and fostering a resilient mindset. Whether they're beginners or seasoned players, our chess program offers a supportive environment where students can challenge themselves, learn from each other, and embark on a journey of self-discovery.

Join us at Baba Smart Genius and Chess Academy, where every lesson is a step towards unleashing the full potential of your mind. Together, let's embark on a transformative journey towards academic excellence, strategic brilliance, and personal growth.
